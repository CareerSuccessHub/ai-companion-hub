## üö® Why This Works Better Than Freemium

### Freemium Problems:
- ‚ùå "Why pay $5/mo for Gemini when Gemini is free?"
- ‚ùå Users will copy prompts to ChatGPT
- ‚ùå No unique value vs free alternatives
- ‚ùå Need 100+ paying users to hit $500/mo
- ‚ùå Customer support overhead

### Affiliate Advantages:
- ‚úÖ No quota limits (everything free = aggressive caching works)
- ‚úÖ Users get value without paying (resume templates, advice)
- ‚úÖ Natural upsell ("Want a pro template? Here's 60% off")
- ‚úÖ Multiple revenue streams (not just one subscription tier)
- ‚úÖ Scales with traffic (not with conversion rate)
- ‚úÖ No customer support for payments/cancellations

**Bottom Line:** 1,000 free users clicking affiliate links > 20 paying subscribers
