# Google Analytics 4 Setup

## Step 1: Create GA4 Account (5 minutes)

1. Go to https://analytics.google.com
2. Click "Start measuring"
3. Account setup:
   - Account name: `AI Student Success Hub`
   - Check all data sharing settings
4. Property setup:
   - Property name: `AI Student Success Hub`
   - Reporting timezone: Your timezone
   - Currency: USD
5. Business details:
   - Industry: Education
   - Business size: Small
6. Business objectives:
   - Check "Generate leads"
   - Check "Examine user behavior"
7. Data stream setup:
   - Choose "Web"
   - Website URL: `https://ai-companion-hub.vercel.app`
   - Stream name: `Main Website`
8. **COPY YOUR MEASUREMENT ID** (format: G-XXXXXXXXXX)
